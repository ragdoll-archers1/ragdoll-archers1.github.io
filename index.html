About Ragdoll Archers

Have you ever seen skilled archers in fighting games? Imagine if they were... stickmen with bodies as soft as noodles and always falling down every time they were hit by an arrow! That's Ragdoll Archers - a unique archery game where every shot is accompanied by laughter. The combination of ragdoll physics and mind-bending battles will keep you glued to your seats.

Weird but extremely attractive gameplay

The gameplay is very simple: you just need to drag the mouse to aim, choose the angle and power of the shot, then release your hand to shoot the arrow. But don't think it's easy - each opponent has their own tactics, and you need to dodge arrows, manage your stamina, and read the flight path to survive. The game has many modes, such as:

Single player: fight continuously with increasingly stronger enemies.

PvP 1v1: fight with friends on the same device.

Co-op 2 players: take down the mischievous enemies together.

Battlefield tactics... comedy

Don't let the silly stickman look fool you. Ragdoll Archers requires precise aiming, smart attack tactics, and reasonable resource management.
Want to win? Do:

Shoot the head to deal massive damage.

Watch the stamina recovery time to shoot continuously without "exhausting".

Take advantage of the health/stamina items that appear randomly in the game.

Arrows of badass arrows: Not just wooden arrows

This is not just a game with normal bows and arrows. Ragdoll Archers owns a whole arsenal of super weird weapons:

Bomb arrows: explode to cause area damage.

Magnetic arrows: pull the enemy towards you.

Electric arrows, poison, balloons, grappling hooks... - each type has its own unique effects and tactics.

Each type of arrow can be unlocked with skulls - the currency collected after each victory.

Upgrade & unlock - Go up from the skull!

After each battle, you will receive skulls – used to:

Increase maximum health.

Expand the stamina bar.

Unlock new names and name slots.

The more you upgrade, the more advantages you have in fierce battles.

Top PvP confrontation – Who is the number 1 archer?

If you think you are a “master shooter”, try PvP mode! You can compete 1v1 with your friends – where every shot is a laugh, and every fall is a great comedy.
Want more fun? Invite more friends, turn on 2-player mode, and overcome crazy attacks from AI together!

Simple graphics – Unbelievably funny effects

Although it is just a simple stickman, Ragdoll Archers brings realistic and funny physical effects:

Fly like an action movie every time you get hit by an arrow.

Fall down when you run out of stamina.

Jerky movements every time you get an electric shock or a magnet.

It is this natural humor that makes the game so engaging and viral in the community.

Ragdoll Archers is the perfect game to relax, have fun, and compete with friends. With simple but deep gameplay, fun graphics, and lively sounds, this is one of the most fun archery games you will ever try.
