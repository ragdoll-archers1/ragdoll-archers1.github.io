About Ragdoll Playground

Imagine being able to drop your ragdolls into a zero-gravity world, create hilarious situations, or even recreate epic explosions using a variety of wacky tools. Ragdoll Playground is where your imagination comes to life – a sandbox world where anything is possible.

A playground without rules

Unlike games with specific tasks, Ragdoll Playground gives you the freedom to do whatever you want. There are no scores. There are no time limits. It’s just you, your ragdoll, and a fun physics world to explore. You control everything – from the objects, tools, to how your ragdoll “takes a beating.”

Unlimited creativity

Want to build a secret base and drop your ragdoll into the middle of an electromagnetic vortex? Or maybe you want to create a system of levers to launch your character into the sky? It’s all possible. Ragdoll Playground is where you can experiment with your craziest ideas without worrying about the consequences – where creativity is king.

Weapons, tools, and ragdolls – The legendary trio

The game's tool arsenal is very rich:

Weapons: Guns, knives, timed bombs, lasers, etc.

Vehicles: Carts, mini planes, moving machines

Objects: Trapdoors, elevators, cranes, crushers

And of course, the main character – the ragdoll – will be the "victim" of all the crazy ideas you create.

Graphics and sounds – Simple but not boring

Although the 2D graphics are quite simple, the realistic physical effects bring a lively and attractive feeling. The collisions, falling movements or explosion effects are all shown very humorously. The sound is gentle but enough for you to clearly feel the interaction of each collision.

You can't help but laugh when you see a ragdoll flying around after a powerful kick, or when a chain reaction of physics causes everything to explode like fireworks. Ragdoll Playground is not only entertaining but also helps you relax and relieve stress after a long, stressful day.
